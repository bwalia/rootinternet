Push Notification

What is A Push Notification?

Similar to an SMS text message, a push notification is an alert that appears instantly on your Android or iOS mobile device. Downtime alerts will be pushed instantly to your mobile device.

Why Push Notification?

Push notifications are an attractive alternative to SMS messages, if you have reliable network connection on your mobile device. We do not charge a per-message fee for each push message, but you will need a plan that supports push notification.

How Does It Work?

     
Pushover notification Prowl notification Pushover notification on Pebble smart watch

 

At this time, we can send push messages to Android and iOS mobile devices with Pushover and Prowl.

  • Install your preferred push notification mobile client using any of following links:
    • Pushover for Android
    • Pushover for iOS
    • Prowl for iOS
  • Add the push contact in your control panel
  • You will receive alerts on your mobile device if your website or server goes down
